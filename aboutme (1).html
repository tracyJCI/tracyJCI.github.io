ABOUT ME

I am a currently a Research Information Specialist for the Canadian Agency for Drugs and Technologies in Health (CADTH).

Before joining CADTH, I worked as an information specialist/librarian for the University of Ottawa Heart Institute in Ottawa (ON), the Maritime SPOR SUPPORT Unit (MSSU) in Halifax (NS), and the Seven Oaks General Hospital Library in Winnipeg (MB).

I am a graduate of the Master of Library and Information Management Studies (MLIS) program at Dalhousie University.
